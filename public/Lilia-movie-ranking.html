The best movies according to Lilia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Matilda

I love Matilda's intelligence, the concept of the gifted child and how happy she can be
at the end of everything. Plus I enjoy watching it because it makes me laugh.

The Invisible Guest

It's a thrilling movie with unexpected twists and an incredible ending

The Conjuring

It's one of the few horror movies that made me jump with fright. With a very interesting plot.

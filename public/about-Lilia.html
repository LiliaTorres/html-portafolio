About Me

My name is Lilia Tamayo, born and currently living in Mérida, Yucatán, Mexico. My academic journey led me to become a Biochemical Engineer with a specialization in biotechnology and a Master of Science in Chemical and Biochemical Sciences. Among my greatest academic and professional achievements are having obtained the highest GPA in my program, the publication of a popular science article, a scientific paper, and the completion of my postgraduate studies. However, my greatest satisfaction comes from my personal growth. The academic path has been a journey of self-discovery—it has allowed me to develop greater self-confidence, manage my emotions effectively, and set personal boundaries. This has truly changed my life for the better.

My love for science is fueled by my innate curiosity and my need to understand the world. Science has given me the means to find answers and make sense of life.

I am characterized as a hyperactive, detail-oriented, analytical, empathetic, sensitive, fun, intelligent, self-critical, proactive, sincere person—and of course, a passionate dancer with a constant desire to acquire new knowledge! At this moment, I am in an exploratory stage, learning to program and improving my English, while working part-time.

My future goals are shared by many: to achieve financial freedom, travel without restrictions, have the freedom to learn whatever interests me, and to start a family.

My life philosophy centers on continuous improvement in every aspect, so I can offer the best of myself to my loved ones and to society.

My message to you is this: always believe in yourself and develop every skill you can, to multiply your chances of success.
